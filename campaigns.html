What We Campaign For:

Anti-Declaw Campaign

Join us in our mission to end the practice of cat declawing. We believe in protecting cats from unnecessary pain and suffering by advocating for legislation to ban declawing procedures. Together, we can ensure that all cats are treated with compassion and respect, preserving their natural behaviors and well-being.

International Collaboration Against Cat Smuggling

Join us in our urgent mission for International Collaboration Against Cat Smuggling. Together, we can protect our feline friends from exploitation and harm. Let's unite across borders to ensure the safety and well-being of all cats, stamping out the cruel practice of smuggling and ensuring they receive the love and care they deserve.

Senior Cat Adoption

Embrace the love and companionship of a senior cat through adoption. These wise and gentle souls have so much to offer, and they deserve a loving home to spend their golden years. Join us in advocating for Senior Cat Adoption and provide a forever home filled with warmth and care, making a difference in the lives of these deserving felines
