Welcome to Snuggly Whisker's Sanctuary, where every feline friend is embraced with open arms to find their purr-fect forever home.

Meet The Whiskers!

Meet our current felines searching for their forever home. When choosing your partner, please consider the needs of each individual cat, so that we can provide the best homes possible for our fluffy friends!

Name:

Piper

Piper is a playful and affectionate, elderly cat in search of their retirement home. She loves to curl up on laps and enjoys chasing after toy mice.

Mittens

Meet Mittens; a playful, affectionate and curious feline with a personality as striking as her coat, awaiting a chance to find her forever home.

Cheesepuff

Introducing Cheesepuff, her striking, fluffy, orange coat being where she got her name.the charmingly plump feline with a passion for relaxation and all things cozy.

Morris

Meet Morris, the epitome of feline charm and charisma. With his striking orange and white coat reminiscent of a vibrant sunset, Morris captivates hearts with his undeniable allure and zesty personality.

Jasper and Willow

Jasper and Willow are a completely healthy and unceperable duo that long for a forever home where they can continue their journey together, side by side, through life's adventures and uncertainties.

Pepper

Meet Pepper, the resilient feline with a heart as strong as her spirit. Pepper faces life with unwavering determination, despite battling with feline diabetes. She navigates each day with courage, inspiring all who know her with her resilience and perseverance.
